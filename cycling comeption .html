About the Tournament

The Annual Cycling Tournament is a thrilling event that brings together cyclists of all ages and skill levels. Compete on scenic routes and enjoy the spirit of competition!

Schedule

  • Day 1: Races (No medals awarded)
  • Day 2: Medal Distribution (Gold, Silver, and Bronze Medals awarded)

Registration

Register now to secure your spot in the tournament! Registration will be open from December 7th to December 10th.

Medals

Medals will be awarded on **Day 2**:

  • Gold Medal: Awarded to the top racer of Day 2.
  • Silver Medal: Awarded to the second-place racer of Day 2.
  • Bronze Medal: Awarded to the third-place racer of Day 2.

Day 1 is dedicated to races, with no medals awarded. Day 2 will feature the medal distribution ceremony with the top three winners receiving their Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals.
